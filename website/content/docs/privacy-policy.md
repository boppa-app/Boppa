# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Boppa is an open source music player. This page explains what data the app
handles and how.

## What Boppa collects

Boppa does not require an account and does not collect or transmit personal
data to any Boppa server. There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no
advertising, and no tracking SDKs in the app.

## What Boppa stores on your device

Your library, likes, playlists, queue, search history, and added media
sources are stored locally on your device. This data stays on your device
and is not sent to Boppa or any third party by the app itself.

## Media source configs

Boppa fetches its bundled app configuration from `cdn.boppa.app`. This
request does not include any personal or account information, only a
request for the current config file.

When you add a media source, Boppa also fetches that config from the URL
you provide. No personal data is attached to that request either.

## Third party websites

Boppa has no built in content catalog. Playback happens through a media
source, a config that points Boppa at a specific website and runs inside an
embedded web view. When you use a media source, you are interacting with
that website directly, including any cookies, logins, or data collection it
performs. Those interactions are governed by the third party site's own
privacy policy, not Boppa's. Boppa does not control, monitor, or receive
data from these sites beyond what is needed to display and play their
content.

If a media source requires you to log in, that login happens inside the
web view for that source, the same as it would in a regular browser tab.
Boppa does not see or store your credentials for third party sites.

## Children's privacy

Boppa is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from
anyone, regardless of age, since it does not collect personal data at all.

## Open source

Boppa's source code is public on
[GitHub](https://github.com/boppa-app/Boppa), so you can review exactly how
the app handles data.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new
"last updated" date.
